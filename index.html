Oaxaca

Oaxaca, located in southwestern Mexico, is a state rich in cultural diversity, biodiversity, and ancestral traditions, known for being home to 16 indigenous ethnic groups and the Afro-Mexican community. It is renowned for its colonial capital, the archaeological site of Monte Albán, its world-famous cuisine, and the iconic Guelaguetza festival
